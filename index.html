About the Game

OpenCarnage is an intense, open-source fighting game that brings unprecedented depth to tactical combat. Combining realistic firearms combat with visceral melee weapon mechanics, OpenCarnage offers players a truly immersive combat experience.

Developed by the community, for the community, this game breaks boundaries with its innovative gameplay and customizable combat system.

Campaign Mode

Engage in a rich, story-driven single-player experience. Navigate through complex missions, each presenting unique challenges and tactical scenarios.

Sandbox Mode

Unleash your creativity with our comprehensive sandbox mode. Experiment with weapons, tactics, and combat scenarios limited only by your imagination.

Multiplayer

Battle against players worldwide in intense, high-stakes multiplayer matches. Test your skills, strategy, and reflexes in real-time combat.
